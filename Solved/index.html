Our Project:

The purpose of this project was to analyze all the different bird species globally and their migration pattern in efforts to perfrom research on a fun topic and as a means of getting away from our real life world problems that is ongoing. To accomplish this analysis, we first pulled data from a csv and organized it and cleaned it up to submit and store in Postgres. The data contains over 100 different bird species including longitude, latitude, and images of each different bird.

After assembling the dataset, we used geomap to plot various longitude and lattitude of each bird origins and it's migration pattern. This site provides the source data and visualizations created as part of the analysis.
